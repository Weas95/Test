Here's a table filled with Csgo Esport teams and there earnings. These are not current. I filled them with some of the legendary linups through the years.
Team Players Age prize money Description
Fnatic Olofmeister 29 $916,819 Considered one of the all time great players. Awared the #1 player of the world in 2015 by HLTV.org and a 2 time major winner.
Fnatic JW 26 $917,361 Jesper Wecksell part of the legendary lineup that won 3 majors. He was the awper of the team and probably one of the most agressive player's for his time. Played very unorthodox and unpredictable. Together with Olofmeister he provided to no fucks attitude Fnatic had and added to their firepower. 3 time major winner.
Fnatic Krimz 27 $916,351 Krimz together with Olofmeister formed one of the best duo's the counter strike world has ever seen. Krimz was the solid reliable rock that would always put up great numbers. Lacking flair to his play he would usually go unnoticed by casters. But once the game finishes Krimz was always found in the top part of the scoreboard. 2 time major winner.
Fnatic Flusha 28 $877,381 Senior Vac himself. Flusha got a lot of hate, many thought he was a cheater earning him the nickname: Senior Vac. To provoke and annoy the haters he would fake mouse movements that looked like aim lock or wall hack. Flusha was also an incredible consistent player that could pump out superstar perfomances when his team needed them most. 3 time major winner and #2 ranked player by HLTV.org in 2014.
Fnatic Pronax 30 $234,151 Pronax was the legendary brain behind the Fnatic machine. Although lacking significant fire power himself he was able to lead his legendary team to three major wins.
Fnatic Dennis 30 $364,125 This table wouldn't be complete without mentioning Dennis. Dennis was the replacement of Pronax once he left the team. While Fnatic wasn't able to win a major with dennis in the team. They did manage to show brilliant flashes of the team they were. Winning some big and significant tournaments.
